about us

Our mission is to ensure a safe environment for all at Government Engineering College,Sreekrishnapuram.We are committed to addressing and resolving any incidents of ragging promptly and ensuring a safe learning environment for all students.

VERBAL

Conduct whether by words,spoken,written or by an act which has the effect of teasing or handling with rudeness


PHYSICAL

Any undisciplined activities by which causes annoyance, hardship, physical or psychological harm


COMPELLING

Asking to do any act which will not in the ordinary course do and which has the effect of causing a sense of shame


DISTURBANCE

Any act by a senior student that prevents, disrupts or disturbs the regular academic activity of any other student or a fresher


EXPLOITATION

Exploiting the services of a fresher for completing the academic tasks assigned to an individual or a group.
