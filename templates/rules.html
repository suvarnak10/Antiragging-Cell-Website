Anti-Ragging Rules

Rules

  • Ragging, in any form, is strictly prohibited within the premises of the institution.
  • No student shall participate, encourage, or abet in any ragging activity.
  • No senior student shall intimidate, harm, or inflict any mental or physical pain on a junior student.
  • Organizing or participating in any event that promotes ragging is strictly prohibited.
  • Engaging in any act that disrupts the peace and harmony of the institution is strictly forbidden.
  • All students are expected to respect the dignity and individuality of their fellow students.
  • No student shall compel or force another student to consume alcohol, drugs, or any other substance.
  • Any act of ragging should be reported immediately to the Anti-Ragging Committee or the authorities.
  • Students found guilty of ragging will face severe disciplinary action, which may include suspension or expulsion.
  • Students are encouraged to create an environment that is free from ragging and promotes a culture of respect and friendship.
